Chocolate Cake

A delicious and easy chocolate cake recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients together.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes.
